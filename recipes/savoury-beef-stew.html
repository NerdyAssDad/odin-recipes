Savoury Beef Stew

There are few things better during a cold winter than hearty and rich beef stew! My recipe borrows from J. Kenji Lopez-Alt's famous recipe, though I do disagree with a few of Kenji's conclusion and my recipe differs as a result. You can find Kenji's recipe here! I highly recommend giving it a read. Kenji is a great resource for recipes general food knowledge.
